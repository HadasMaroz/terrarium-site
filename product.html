Light

Bright, indirect light. Never direct sun — glass focuses heat. North or east windows are perfect.

Water

Sealed pieces recycle their own rain and need no watering. Open pieces take a light mist once a month.

Trimming & life span

Growth is slow by design. If a fern touches the glass, open, trim, reseal — five minutes, twice a year. A balanced vessel lives for decades.

Shipping

Each world ships planted, cushioned in biodegradable foam, with a care card. Free shipping over $200.
