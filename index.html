The Maker

Hi, I'm Shlomi!

Shlomi Meroz is a dedicated father of three boys and a passionate creator with a deep love for nature and craftsmanship. Balancing the energetic, joyful chaos of raising three young sons with his creative pursuits, Shlomi finds his perfect rhythm in both family life and his unique hands-on hobbies.

When he isn’t busy being a hands-on dad, Shlomi channels his patience and attention to detail into building beautiful, custom terrariums. What started as a fascinating hobby has grown into a true art form for him—designing self-sustaining miniature ecosystems encased in glass. Each terrarium is a reflection of his appreciation for nature, combining layers of earth, moss, and delicate greenery to create a peaceful, living piece of art.

Whether he is navigating the adventures of fatherhood or meticulously sculpting a tiny green world, Shlomi brings creativity, dedication, and a thoughtful touch to everything he does.
